inversewsb.fund is a simulated actively-managed mutual fund that attempts to buy and sell individual securities based on posts made on r/wallstreetbets, a popular internet forum used to discuss potential stock trades.

using PRAW and the NLTK, posts are constantly scraped and analyzed for their sentiment. since it is a widely held belief that simply inverting the trades being discussed can lead to great profits, "bullish" posts that mention a particular security trigger the sale of that security, while "bearish" posts trigger the purchase of said security.

Alpaca provides the paper-trading account and API used to intitiate the simulated trades/transactions.
